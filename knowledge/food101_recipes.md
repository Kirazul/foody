# Food-101 Dish Recipes & Nutritional Database

This document contains detailed recipe guidelines, nutritional estimates, macro breakdowns, and healthy modifications for the 101 food classes in the Food-101 dataset. Use this private context as grounding information when responding to user requests.

# Apple Pie
- **Est. Calories**: 300 - 450 kcal per slice (120g)
- **Est. Macros**: Carbs: 50-65g | Protein: 3-5g | Fat: 12-18g
- **Core Ingredients**: Apples (Granny Smith), Flour, Butter, Sugar, Cinnamon, Nutmeg, Pie crust
- **Healthy Swaps**: Use stevia or erythritol instead of sugar; use a whole-wheat crust or crumble topping with oat flour to reduce fat and increase fiber.
- **Quick Cooking Guide**: Peel, core, and slice apples. Toss with sugar, cinnamon, nutmeg, and a little flour. Place into a pie shell, dot with butter, cover with top crust, slice steam vents, and bake at 400°F (200°C) for 45 minutes.

# Baby Back Ribs
- **Est. Calories**: 600 - 900 kcal per half rack (300g)
- **Est. Macros**: Carbs: 10-25g | Protein: 45-55g | Fat: 45-60g
- **Core Ingredients**: Pork back ribs, BBQ sauce, Brown sugar, Paprika, Garlic powder, Onion powder, Salt, Black pepper
- **Healthy Swaps**: Use a sugar-free BBQ sauce and honey substitute; bake or grill instead of deep-frying; trim excess visible fat before cooking.
- **Quick Cooking Guide**: Remove membrane from back of ribs. Rub with a mix of brown sugar, paprika, garlic powder, salt, and pepper. Wrap tightly in foil and bake at 275°F (135°C) for 3 hours. Unwrap, brush with BBQ sauce, and broil for 5 minutes.

# Baklava
- **Est. Calories**: 280 - 400 kcal per piece (80g)
- **Est. Macros**: Carbs: 40-55g | Protein: 4-6g | Fat: 15-22g
- **Core Ingredients**: Phyllo dough, Walnuts, Pistachios, Butter, Honey, Sugar, Cinnamon, Lemon juice
- **Healthy Swaps**: Reduce butter by mixing with light cooking spray; use sugar-free honey/syrup alternatives to lower simple sugar content.
- **Quick Cooking Guide**: Layer buttered sheets of phyllo dough in a pan, spreading chopped nuts mixed with cinnamon between layers. Cut into diamonds. Bake at 350°F (175°C) for 50 minutes. Pour cooled honey-sugar syrup over hot baklava immediately.

# Beef Carpaccio
- **Est. Calories**: 150 - 250 kcal per serving (120g)
- **Est. Macros**: Carbs: 1-3g | Protein: 20-25g | Fat: 8-15g
- **Core Ingredients**: Beef tenderloin, Olive oil, Lemon juice, Parmesan cheese, Arugula, Capers, Salt, Pepper
- **Healthy Swaps**: Drizzle with high-quality balsamic vinegar instead of excess oil; use low-fat parmesan flakes or nutritional yeast.
- **Quick Cooking Guide**: Sear beef tenderloin briefly on all sides, then freeze for 1 hour to firm. Slice paper-thin and arrange on a plate. Top with arugula, capers, parmesan shavings, olive oil, lemon juice, salt, and pepper.

# Beef Tartare
- **Est. Calories**: 200 - 320 kcal per serving (150g)
- **Est. Macros**: Carbs: 2-5g | Protein: 25-30g | Fat: 12-20g
- **Core Ingredients**: Raw beef fillet (minced), Egg yolk, Dijon mustard, Shallots, Capers, Parsley, Worcestershire sauce, Tabasco
- **Healthy Swaps**: Serve with sliced cucumbers or endive leaves instead of toast points to eliminate refined carbohydrates.
- **Quick Cooking Guide**: Finely mince high-quality raw beef fillet. Mix with minced shallots, capers, parsley, mustard, Worcestershire sauce, and Tabasco. Shape into a mound, make an indentation on top, and place a raw egg yolk in it.

# Beet Salad

- **Core Ingredients**: Beets, Goat cheese, Walnuts, Arugula, Olive oil, Balsamic vinegar, Honey, Salt
- **Healthy Swaps**: Substitute goat cheese with low-fat feta or cottage cheese; use toasted sunflower seeds instead of walnuts to reduce fat.

# Beignets
- **Est. Calories**: 350 - 500 kcal per serving of 3
- **Est. Macros**: Carbs: 50-65g | Protein: 5-7g | Fat: 15-25g
- **Core Ingredients**: Flour, Yeast, Sugar, Evaporated milk, Shortening, Eggs, Powdered sugar, Oil for frying
- **Healthy Swaps**: Air-fry beignets instead of deep-frying; dust lightly with powdered stevia instead of heaps of powdered sugar.
- **Quick Cooking Guide**: Dissolve yeast in warm water with sugar. Mix in milk, eggs, melted shortening, and flour. Rise for 2 hours. Roll dough, cut into squares, and deep-fry in 360°F (180°C) oil until puffed and golden. Dust heavily with powdered sugar.

- **Est. Macros**: Carbs: 4-8g | Protein: 22-26g | Fat: 8-18g
- **Core Ingredients**: Sea scallops, Butter, Olive oil, Garlic, Lemon juice, Salt, Parsley
- **Healthy Swaps**: Pan-sear scallops using olive oil spray instead of heavy butter; serve over cauliflower mash.
- **Quick Cooking Guide**: Pat scallops dry and season with salt. Heat oil and butter in a skillet until smoking. Sear scallops for 2 minutes on one side until crusty, flip and cook for 1 minute. Add garlic, lemon juice, baste, and serve.

# Seaweed Salad
- **Est. Calories**: 80 - 150 kcal per bowl (100g)
- **Est. Macros**: Carbs: 10-15g | Protein: 1-3g | Fat: 4-8g
- **Core Ingredients**: Dried wakame seaweed, Soy sauce, Rice vinegar, Sesame oil, Sesame seeds, Sugar, Chili flakes
- **Healthy Swaps**: Decrease sesame oil and sugar quantities to lower calories and simple sugars.
- **Quick Cooking Guide**: Rehydrate dried wakame in water for 10 minutes. Squeeze out excess water. Whisk soy sauce, vinegar, sesame oil, sugar, sesame seeds, and chili flakes. Toss seaweed in dressing and chill.

# Shrimp And Grits
- **Est. Calories**: 450 - 700 kcal per bowl
- **Est. Macros**: Carbs: 45-60g | Protein: 25-35g | Fat: 20-35g
- **Core Ingredients**: Shrimp, Stone-ground grits, Milk, Cheese, Bacon, Garlic, Bell peppers, Butter, Green onions
- **Healthy Swaps**: Cook grits in water or skim milk instead of cream; use turkey bacon; use low-fat cheddar; sauté shrimp in olive oil spray.
- **Quick Cooking Guide**: Cook grits in boiling salted water and milk, stir in cheese. Cook bacon in a skillet, sauté peppers, onions, garlic, and shrimp in grease. Spoon hot grits into bowl, top with shrimp mixture and crumbled bacon.

# Spaghetti Bolognese
- **Est. Calories**: 450 - 650 kcal per plate
- **Est. Macros**: Carbs: 60-75g | Protein: 25-35g | Fat: 12-22g
- **Core Ingredients**: Spaghetti, Ground beef, Canned crushed tomatoes, Onions, Carrots, Celery, Garlic, Olive oil, Tomato paste, Red wine, Oregano, Parmesan
- **Healthy Swaps**: Use whole-wheat spaghetti or zucchini noodles (zoodles); swap ground beef for ground turkey or brown lentils; limit cheese garnish.
- **Quick Cooking Guide**: Sauté chopped onion, garlic, carrot, and celery. Add beef and brown. Pour in red wine, then tomato paste, crushed tomatoes, and oregano. Simmer for 45 mins. Boil pasta, toss with sauce, and garnish with parmesan.

# Spaghetti Carbonara
- **Est. Calories**: 550 - 800 kcal per plate
- **Est. Macros**: Carbs: 55-70g | Protein: 25-35g | Fat: 25-42g
- **Core Ingredients**: Spaghetti, Pancetta or Guanciale, Egg yolks, Pecorino Romano, Parmesan, Black pepper, Garlic
- **Healthy Swaps**: Use turkey bacon; replace half the pasta with spaghetti squash or zoodles; reduce cheese portion.
- **Quick Cooking Guide**: Render pancetta in a skillet. Whisk eggs, yolks, grated Pecorino, and pepper in a bowl. Boil pasta al dente. Drain, saving water. Toss pasta with hot pancetta, remove from heat, quickly stir in egg mixture, adding water as needed to emulsify.

# Spring Rolls
- **Est. Calories**: 150 - 280 kcal per serving of 2 fried rolls
- **Est. Macros**: Carbs: 25-35g | Protein: 4-6g | Fat: 8-15g
- **Core Ingredients**: Spring roll wrappers, Cabbage, Carrots, Mushrooms, Glass noodles, Garlic, Soy sauce, Oil for frying
- **Healthy Swaps**: Make fresh summer rolls using rice paper (unfried), raw carrots, cucumbers, herbs, and shrimp.
- **Quick Cooking Guide**: Sauté shredded cabbage, carrots, mushrooms, garlic, and soy sauce. Soak noodles and mix. Cool filling, place on wrappers, roll, and seal. Deep fry until golden brown, or bake with light oil spray.

# Steak
- **Est. Calories**: 400 - 700 kcal per steak (250g depending on cut)
- **Est. Macros**: Carbs: 0g | Protein: 45-60g | Fat: 20-50g
- **Core Ingredients**: Ribeye or New York Strip steak, Olive oil, Butter, Garlic, Rosemary, Salt, Black pepper
- **Healthy Swaps**: Choose a leaner cut like sirloin or flank steak; grill or broil without butter basting.
- **Quick Cooking Guide**: Season steak with salt and pepper. Heat oil in skillet until very hot. Sear steak for 3-4 minutes per side. Baste with butter, garlic, and rosemary in the last minute. Rest for 5 minutes before slicing.

# Strawberry Shortcake
- **Est. Calories**: 300 - 450 kcal per serving
- **Est. Macros**: Carbs: 45-60g | Protein: 4-6g | Fat: 12-20g
- **Core Ingredients**: Sweet biscuits (flour, sugar, butter), Strawberries, Sugar, Heavy cream, Vanilla
- **Healthy Swaps**: Use low-fat yogurt instead of whipped cream; sweeten strawberries with stevia; use a whole-wheat flour biscuit recipe.
- **Quick Cooking Guide**: Slice strawberries and toss with sugar, let sit to release juices. Whip heavy cream with vanilla. Cut baked biscuits in half, layer sweetened strawberries, juices, and whipped cream, then cover with top half.

# Sushi
- **Est. Calories**: 280 - 450 kcal per roll (8 pieces)
- **Est. Macros**: Carbs: 45-65g | Protein: 12-18g | Fat: 4-12g
- **Core Ingredients**: Sushi rice, Nori seaweed sheets, Raw fish (tuna/salmon), Cucumber, Avocado, Soy sauce, Wasabi, Pickled ginger
- **Healthy Swaps**: Choose sashimi (rice-free) or naruto rolls (wrapped in cucumber); avoid tempura fried toppings and spicy mayo/cream cheese fillings.
- **Quick Cooking Guide**: Spread cooked seasoned sushi rice over nori sheet. Lay strips of fish, cucumber, and avocado in center. Roll tightly using a bamboo mat. Slice into 8 pieces and serve with soy sauce, wasabi, and ginger.

# Tacos
- **Est. Calories**: 350 - 550 kcal per serving of 3 tacos
- **Est. Macros**: Carbs: 30-45g | Protein: 20-30g | Fat: 15-25g
- **Core Ingredients**: Corn tortillas, Ground beef or chicken, Taco seasoning, Lettuce, Cheese, Salsa, Sour cream
- **Healthy Swaps**: Use lean ground turkey, black beans, or grilled chicken; use soft corn tortillas instead of fried shells; swap sour cream for Greek yogurt.
- **Quick Cooking Guide**: Brown meat and simmer with taco seasoning. Warm corn tortillas. Fill tortillas with meat, shredded lettuce, diced tomatoes, salsa, a sprinkle of cheese, and Greek yogurt.

# Takoyaki
- **Est. Calories**: 250 - 380 kcal per serving of 6 balls
- **Est. Macros**: Carbs: 35-48g | Protein: 8-12g | Fat: 8-15g
- **Core Ingredients**: Flour, Dashi broth, Egg, Octopus pieces, Pickled ginger, Green onions, Takoyaki sauce, Japanese mayo, Bonito flakes
- **Healthy Swaps**: Limit mayonnaise drizzle; increase cabbage or green onions in batter; cook in pan with minimal oil spray.
- **Quick Cooking Guide**: Whisk flour, dashi, and egg to make batter. Pour into hot oiled takoyaki pan cavities. Drop octopus, ginger, and scallions in. Flip balls using picks as they cook to form spheres. Top with sauce, mayo, bonito.

# Tiramisu
- **Est. Calories**: 350 - 500 kcal per slice (120g)
- **Est. Macros**: Carbs: 35-50g | Protein: 5-8g | Fat: 20-32g
- **Core Ingredients**: Ladyfingers, Espresso, Mascarpone cheese, Egg yolks, Sugar, Heavy cream, Cocoa powder, Kahlua or rum
- **Healthy Swaps**: Use light mascarpone or blend cream cheese with Greek yogurt; substitute sugar with stevia; use decaf espresso if preferred.
- **Quick Cooking Guide**: Whisk egg yolks and sugar. Fold in whipped mascarpone and cream. Dip ladyfingers briefly in espresso mixed with rum. Layer dipped ladyfingers and cream mixture in a dish. Dust heavily with cocoa, chill for 6 hours.

# Tuna Tartare
- **Est. Calories**: 150 - 250 kcal per serving (120g)
- **Est. Macros**: Carbs: 4-8g | Protein: 22-26g | Fat: 6-12g
- **Core Ingredients**: Sushi-grade tuna, Avocado, Soy sauce, Sesame oil, Ginger, Lime juice, Green onions, Sesame seeds
- **Healthy Swaps**: Serve with sliced cucumbers or baked wonton chips instead of deep-fried options; keep sesame oil minimal.
- **Quick Cooking Guide**: Finely dice sushi-grade tuna and avocado. Toss tuna with soy sauce, sesame oil, lime juice, ginger, and scallions. Layer diced avocado in mold, top with tuna mixture, press gently, and unmold. Garnish with sesame seeds.

# Waffles
- **Est. Calories**: 300 - 500 kcal per waffle
- **Est. Macros**: Carbs: 45-65g | Protein: 6-10g | Fat: 10-18g
- **Core Ingredients**: Flour, Milk, Eggs, Butter or oil, Baking powder, Sugar, Vanilla
- **Healthy Swaps**: Use whole-wheat or oat flour; use egg whites; replace sugar with monk fruit; top with fresh blueberries and sugar-free syrup.
- **Quick Cooking Guide**: Whisk flour, baking powder, salt, and sugar. Beat egg whites to stiff peaks. Mix yolks, milk, vanilla, and melted butter. Stir wet into dry, then fold in whites. Bake in preheated waffle iron until crispy.
